Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our platform

How current are your past questions?

We update our question bank annually to include the most recent exam papers. Our current collection includes questions from 2010 to 2023 for all major exams, with 2024 papers being added as soon as they're released.

Can I access the materials offline?

Yes! Our mobile app allows you to download materials for offline access. You can study anywhere, anytime without needing an internet connection. Downloaded materials sync automatically when you're back online.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with our service. After accessing more than 30% of the materials, refunds are prorated based on usage. Institutional purchases may have different refund policies.

How do the live classes work?

Our live classes are interactive sessions conducted via Zoom or our custom virtual classroom. You can ask questions in real-time, participate in discussions, and get immediate feedback. All classes are recorded and available for later viewing if you miss a session.

Are your tutors qualified?

Absolutely! All our tutors are certified teachers with at least 5 years of experience preparing students for Nigerian exams. Many are former examiners or have advanced degrees in their subject areas. We conduct thorough background checks and ongoing performance evaluations.
